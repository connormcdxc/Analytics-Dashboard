INST 490 Capstone - Team B
University of Maryland
Spring 2020

This website was created as a final project by a group of students at UMD's iSchool participating in INST 490. INST 490 is a capstone course required for graduation, and is intended to produce a project that showcases student skills conveying learned and employable skills gained while studying at UMD.

Given the task of creating a dynamic dashboard that utilizes data generated by another group in the class, our team did so using agile methodology. Notably, the course started before measures were taken in the United States to stifle Covid-19; and the course started out with weekly in-person lectures and shifted to an online learning environment before the halfway mark of the semester. Having to adapt to new settings, our group and class adapted by adopting remote procedures for interaction and collaboration.

The purpose of this dashboard is to allow whoever uses it to see just who in the team and/or company is participating and who is not really working together with the other members of a team. The data used in the dashboard comes in the form of messages and the actors who act upon them. With this data, we create graphics that reflect the given data. The given data is the amount of messages each actor participates in, along with the action they perform in that message.
